# Namuna 26 — मासिक विवरण (Monthly Returns: 26क Income + 26ख Expenditure)

> **Two physical sub-forms:** 26क (जमा — Income) and 26ख (खर्च — Expenditure) submitted together monthly.

## Purpose
Two mandatory monthly returns giving the PS a real-time financial monitoring window into each GP. 26क = income by account head for the month. 26ख = expenditure by account head. Together they enable the PS to detect budget overruns, non-collection, and financial irregularities monthly.

## Key Fields
**N26क (Income):** Month/year; Account head; Budget provision; Receipts this month; Cumulative April to date; Balance to collect; Certification
**N26ख (Expenditure):** Month/year; Account head; Budget provision; Expenditure this month; Cumulative April to date; Balance available; Certification; Submitted date

## Validation Rules
- N26क monthly totals must agree with N5 receipt side monthly closing
- N26ख monthly totals must agree with N5 payment side monthly closing
- Late submission (after 15th) is an audit objection
- Non-submission for 3+ months triggers PS intervention
- Head-wise expenditure must not exceed budget provision — overspend visible here
- 12-month cumulative must equal N3 annual totals at year-end

## Audit Risk
HIGH — Common objections:
- Returns not submitted for months
- Totals in N26 discrepant with N5 cash book
- Head-wise overspend visible in N26 but no corrective action taken by GP
