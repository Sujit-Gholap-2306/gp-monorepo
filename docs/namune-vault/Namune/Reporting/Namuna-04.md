## Purpose
Records the GP's financial position — all assets and liabilities as at year-end. The GP equivalent of a balance sheet. Prepared alongside N3 (Annual Statement) for the Gram Sabha.

## Key Fields
**ASSETS:** Cash in hand; Bank balances; Investments (N25); Advances recoverable (N17); Stock in hand; Movable assets (N16); Immovable property (N22); Roads (N23); Land (N24); Trees (N33)
**LIABILITIES:** Loans outstanding (N29); Security deposits payable (N17); Refunds payable (N32); Outstanding creditors
- Net assets; Previous year comparative; Certifications

**Feeds Into:** *(none — terminal document)*

## Validation Rules
- Cash in hand per N4 must agree with N5 closing balance
- Bank balances must agree with bank statement / reconciliation
- Investment total = N25; Loan outstanding = N29
- Assets total − Liabilities total = Fund balance (carries forward to N1 next year)

## Audit Risk
MEDIUM — Common objections:
- Cash balance per N4 not matching N5 closing balance
- Asset values not updated (same figures for years)
- Loans not appearing as liabilities
