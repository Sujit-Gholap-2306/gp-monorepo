## Purpose
Records travel allowance claims by GP employees and elected members for official travel — inspections, training, official meetings at Block/District level. Each claim requires supporting documentation.

## Key Fields
- Claim number; Employee name and designation (from N13)
- Dates of travel; Origin → Destination → Return; Purpose
- Mode of travel; Distance (km); Fare claimed (tickets attached)
- Daily allowance (DA) per day; Total claim amount
- Sanctioning authority signature; Payment date and N5 reference
- Employee acknowledgment signature

## Dependencies
**Depends On:** [[Namuna-13]]
**Feeds Into:** [[Namuna-05]] [[Namuna-26]]

## Validation Rules
- TA only for officially sanctioned travel — personal travel not reimbursable
- Actual tickets/receipts must be attached — claimed amount ≤ actual fare
- Prescribed class of travel per grade must be followed [VERIFY]
- Claims submitted after prescribed period require sanction for late submission
- Sarpanch sumptuary allowance (§33A) is separate — must not be mixed with TA
- Total TA within N1 provision under TA/contingency head

## Audit Risk
LOW — Common objections:
- TA claims without actual travel proof
- Claims submitted well after prescribed period without sanction
- Fare claimed exceeds permissible class
