# Namuna 23 — ताब्यातील रस्त्याची नोंदवही (Roads Register)

## Purpose
Records all roads under GP control — length, width, surface type, and maintenance history. Roads are significant GP assets and a major category of works expenditure.

## Key Fields
- Road number/name; From–To points
- Total length (km); Width (m); Surface type (kutcha/gravel/WBM/CC/BT)
- Date of construction/handover to GP; Construction cost/value
- Works register reference (N20 work no.)
- Last maintenance date and N20 work number; Maintenance cost
- Present condition; Remarks

## Dependencies
**Depends On:** [[Namuna-20]]
**Feeds Into:** [[Namuna-04]] [[Namuna-22]]

## Validation Rules
- Roads constructed under schemes must be in this register — missing scheme roads are objections
- Maintenance expenditure (from N20) must be cross-referenced here
- Roads handed over from PWD/ZP must have proper transfer documentation

## Audit Risk
MEDIUM — Common objections:
- Roads constructed under PMGSY/scheme not entered
- Maintenance not recorded for years — roads deteriorating without action documented
